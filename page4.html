PQ4R Method

The PQ4R Method takes an active approach to learning that improves memorization and understanding of the topic.

Preview: Preview the information before you start reading to get an idea of the subject.

Question: Ask yourself questions related to the topic.

Read: Read the information one section at a time and try to identify answers to your questions.

Reflect: Did you answer all the questions?

Recite: In your own words, either speak or write down a summary of the information you just read.

Review: Look over the material one more time and answer any questions that have not yet been answered.
