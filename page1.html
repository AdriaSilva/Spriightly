Feynman Technique

The Feyman Technique is an efficient method of learing a concept quickly by explaining it in plain and simple terms. This concept works towards explaining a concept in our own words, we are likely to understand it faster.

Step 1: Write the subject you are studying at the top of the sheet.

Step 2: Explain it in your own words.

Step 3: Review what you wrote and identify any areas where you were wrong.

Step 4: If you used technical terms or complex langauge, go back and rewrite these in simpler terms.
